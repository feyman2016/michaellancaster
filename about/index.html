About me

Hi! I'm Michael Lancaster. 25. born and raised in Brasil with dual citizenship (USA and Brasil).
Following my dreams I ended up at the land of opportunity (USA) 4 years ago.

I'm a UI/Front End engineer (occasionally full-stack) highly motivated and passionate about software development and open source projects involving Web Applications, SPA's, mobile development and Hybrid Apps (desktop/mobile).

Currently working as Software Engineer at Uptake, previously I worked at Isobar (Senior Developer), Orbitz (UI Engineer) and Leo Burnett (Associate Web Engineer).

I have extensive experience working in both environments, Product and Agency where I've worked in close proximity to product, design and software engineers.

My motivation to learn new things and work exposure set leans heavily towards all things Front End, HTML5, CSS3 (SASS/LESS - Design Patterns), Javascript and it's most known libs/frameworks/Tools (Angular/jQuery/Backbone/React/Grunt/NW.js/PhoneGap...), Responsive design and cross-browser/platform development.

I also love working with Node.js/npm ecosystem and the whole tech stack Back/Front including DevOps tasks.

On my spare time when if not working on my side projects like Soundnode App to support Soundcloud for Desktop (Win/Mac/Linux) I try to contribute to open source projects Github otherwise practicing sports/lifting weights.
